Telemedicine has transformed how we access healthcare, making it possible to consult with experienced doctors without leaving home. At Lumea Health, we've seen a 300% increase in telemedicine consultations since 2023, and patient satisfaction ratings remain above 95%.

## When Telemedicine Works Best

Virtual consultations are ideal for:
- Follow-up appointments for known conditions
- Prescription renewals and medication reviews
- Discussing test results
- Mild respiratory infections and skin concerns (via photo)
- Mental health consultations
- Sick leave certificates

## How to Prepare

- Find a quiet, well-lit space
- Have a list of your symptoms, medications, and questions ready
- Test your camera and microphone before the call
- If relevant, have any recent test results accessible

## After Your Consultation

Your doctor can electronically send prescriptions to any pharmacy in Poland, issue referrals, and order laboratory tests — all without an in-person visit.
