## Our Flagship Clinic

Located in the heart of Warsaw on Marszałkowska Street, Lumea Health Centrum is our largest and most comprehensive facility. With over 20 consulting rooms, a full diagnostic laboratory, and imaging center, this clinic offers the complete range of Lumea Health services.

## Facilities

- 22 consulting rooms across two floors
- In-house laboratory with same-day results
- Digital X-ray and ultrasound suite
- Rehabilitation center with gym
- Private parking for patients
- Wheelchair accessible

## Getting Here

The clinic is located at ul. Marszałkowska 84, easily accessible by metro (Politechnika station, 5-minute walk), multiple bus and tram lines, and with dedicated patient parking available.
