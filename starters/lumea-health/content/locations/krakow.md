## Healthcare in the Heart of Kraków

Situated on ul. Dietla, just minutes from Kraków's historic center, our Kraków clinic offers a wide range of medical services with a focus on orthopedics and rehabilitation. The clinic features a modern rehabilitation center with specialized equipment for post-surgical and sports recovery.

## Facilities

- 14 consulting rooms
- Rehabilitation center with hydrotherapy
- In-house laboratory
- Digital imaging (X-ray, ultrasound)
- Underground parking
