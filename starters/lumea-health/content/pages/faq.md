## Appointments

**How do I book an appointment?**
You can book online through our patient portal, call any clinic directly, or use our contact form. Same-day appointments are often available for primary care.

**Can I see a specialist without a referral?**
Yes. As a private clinic, Lumea Health does not require a GP referral for specialist consultations. You can book directly with any specialist.

**What is your cancellation policy?**
We request at least 24 hours' notice for cancellations. Late cancellations may incur a 50% fee.

## Insurance & Payment

**Do you accept health insurance?**
Yes, we accept Medicover, Luxmed, Enel-Med, PZU Zdrowie, and several other insurance plans. Please check with your insurer for coverage details.

**What payment methods do you accept?**
Cash, credit/debit cards, and bank transfers. We also offer installment plans for larger procedures.

## Medical Records

**Can I access my medical records online?**
Yes, all Lumea Health patients have access to our secure patient portal where you can view test results, appointment history, and medical records.

**How do I transfer my records from another clinic?**
Contact our reception desk and we'll coordinate the transfer of your medical documentation.

## Telemedicine

**What do I need for a telemedicine appointment?**
A device with a camera and microphone, a stable internet connection, and a quiet, private space. We use a secure, GDPR-compliant video platform.
