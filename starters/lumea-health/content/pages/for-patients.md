## Before Your Visit

Please bring the following to your appointment:
- Valid ID document
- Health insurance card (if applicable)
- List of current medications
- Previous medical records related to your visit
- Referral letter (if you have one, though not required)

## Your First Visit

Your first consultation at Lumea Health typically takes 30-45 minutes. Your doctor will:
1. Review your medical history
2. Perform a thorough examination
3. Discuss diagnosis and treatment options
4. Arrange any necessary tests or referrals
5. Provide a written summary of the visit

## Patient Rights

As a Lumea Health patient, you have the right to:
- Access your complete medical records
- Receive clear information about your diagnosis and treatment
- Make informed decisions about your care
- Confidentiality of your medical information
- File a complaint if dissatisfied with any aspect of care
