## Why Lumea Health?

Lumea Health is growing, and we're looking for talented healthcare professionals who share our commitment to patient-centered care. We offer competitive compensation, modern facilities, and a supportive work environment.

## What We Offer

- Competitive salaries and performance bonuses
- Flexible scheduling options
- Continuous medical education funding
- Modern, well-equipped clinics
- Collaborative, multidisciplinary teams
- Private health insurance for employees and families

## Open Positions

We're currently hiring across all locations:

- **Cardiologist** — Warsaw Centrum (full-time)
- **General Practitioner** — Poznań (full-time or part-time)
- **Pediatric Nurse** — Warsaw Ursynów (full-time)
- **Physiotherapist** — Kraków (full-time)
- **Medical Receptionist** — Multiple locations
- **Telemedicine Physician** — Remote (part-time)

## How to Apply

Send your CV and a brief cover letter to **careers@lumeahealth.pl** with the position title in the subject line. We aim to respond to all applications within 5 business days.
