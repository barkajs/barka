## Hormonal & Metabolic Health

Our endocrinology department specializes in the diagnosis and management of hormonal disorders and metabolic conditions. We take a holistic approach, combining medical treatment with lifestyle modifications for optimal outcomes.

## Services

- **Diabetes Management** — Type 1 and Type 2 diabetes care, insulin pump therapy, CGM support
- **Thyroid Disorders** — Hypothyroidism, hyperthyroidism, thyroid nodules, and thyroid cancer follow-up
- **Metabolic Syndrome** — Weight management and cardiovascular risk reduction
- **Hormonal Imbalances** — Adrenal disorders, pituitary conditions, and hormonal testing
- **Osteoporosis Management** — Bone density assessment and treatment
- **Polycystic Ovary Syndrome** — Comprehensive PCOS management in collaboration with gynecology
