## Comprehensive Skin Care

Our dermatology department provides both medical and aesthetic skin care services. Using advanced dermatoscopy and imaging technology, we offer precise diagnostics and evidence-based treatments for all skin conditions.

## Medical Dermatology

- **Skin Cancer Screening** — Full-body dermoscopic examination with digital documentation
- **Acne Treatment** — Personalized treatment plans including topical and systemic therapies
- **Eczema & Psoriasis Management** — Long-term management strategies and biologic therapies
- **Allergic Skin Conditions** — Patch testing and allergy management
- **Skin Infections** — Diagnosis and treatment of bacterial, viral, and fungal skin conditions
- **Mole Monitoring** — Digital dermoscopy with AI-assisted pattern recognition

## Aesthetic Dermatology

- Botox and dermal fillers
- Chemical peels
- Laser treatments for scars and pigmentation
- Microneedling and PRP therapy

## Why Choose Lumea Health Dermatology

Our dermatologists are board-certified specialists with training from leading European institutions. We use state-of-the-art equipment including digital dermatoscopes and UV photography for precise diagnosis.
