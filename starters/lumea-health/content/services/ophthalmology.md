## Expert Eye Care

Our ophthalmology department offers comprehensive eye examinations and treatment for a wide range of conditions. From routine vision checks to complex disease management, our specialists use the latest diagnostic equipment.

## Services

- **Comprehensive Eye Exams** — Complete assessment of visual acuity and eye health
- **Glaucoma Screening** — Intraocular pressure measurement and optic nerve evaluation
- **Diabetic Eye Screening** — Retinal photography and OCT scanning
- **Cataract Assessment** — Evaluation and surgical referrals
- **Pediatric Ophthalmology** — Children's eye health and vision development
- **Contact Lens Fitting** — Professional fitting and follow-up care
