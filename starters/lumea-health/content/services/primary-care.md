## Your Health Partner

Lumea Health's primary care physicians serve as your first point of contact for all health concerns. We provide thorough consultations, coordinate specialist referrals, and manage ongoing health conditions with a personal touch.

## What We Offer

- **General Consultations** — Comprehensive health assessments and follow-ups
- **Preventive Health Screenings** — Age-appropriate screening programs
- **Chronic Disease Management** — Diabetes, hypertension, asthma, and more
- **Sick Visits** — Same-day appointments for acute conditions
- **Travel Medicine** — Pre-travel consultations and vaccinations
- **Health Certificates** — Driving licenses, fitness certificates, and more
