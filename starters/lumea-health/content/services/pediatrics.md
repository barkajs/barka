## Caring for Your Child's Health

Lumea Health's pediatric department provides expert care for children from birth through age 18. Our child-friendly clinics are designed to make medical visits a positive experience for both children and parents.

## Our Pediatric Services

- **Well-Child Visits** — Regular developmental check-ups at every milestone
- **Vaccination Programs** — Complete immunization schedules following Polish National Immunization Programme
- **Acute Illness Care** — Same-day appointments for childhood illnesses
- **Developmental Assessments** — Speech, motor, and cognitive development screening
- **Allergy Testing** — Pediatric allergy diagnostics and management
- **Adolescent Health** — Confidential care for teenagers

## Child-Friendly Environment

Our pediatric waiting rooms feature play areas, age-appropriate reading materials, and trained staff who specialize in putting young patients at ease. We allocate extra time for pediatric appointments to ensure thorough examinations without rushing.
