## Vaccination Services

Lumea Health provides the full range of vaccinations for children and adults, following the Polish National Immunization Programme and WHO recommendations.

## Available Vaccines

- **Childhood Immunizations** — Complete schedule from birth to 18 years
- **Adult Boosters** — Tetanus, pertussis, MMR, and hepatitis
- **Travel Vaccines** — Yellow fever, hepatitis A/B, typhoid, rabies, and more
- **Seasonal Flu** — Annual influenza vaccination campaigns
- **HPV Vaccination** — For both girls and boys aged 9-26
- **COVID-19** — Current booster recommendations
