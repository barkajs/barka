## Workplace Health

Lumea Health provides complete occupational medicine services for employers and employees. We handle all statutory medical examinations required under Polish labor law.

## Services

- **Pre-Employment Examinations** — Medical fitness assessments for new hires
- **Periodic Health Checks** — Mandatory health surveillance as required by law
- **Return-to-Work Assessments** — Fitness evaluations after illness or injury
- **Health & Safety Consultations** — Workplace risk assessment support
- **Corporate Health Programs** — Tailored wellness programs for organizations
- **Vaccination Programs** — Flu shots and travel vaccinations for companies
