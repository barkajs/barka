## Women's Health Services

Our gynecology department provides compassionate, comprehensive care for women at every stage of life. From adolescent health through menopause management, our specialists offer personalized attention in a comfortable, private setting.

## Our Gynecology Services

- **Preventive Screenings** — Pap smears, HPV testing, and breast examinations
- **Prenatal Care** — Complete pregnancy monitoring with 3D/4D ultrasound
- **Fertility Consultations** — Initial assessment and treatment planning
- **Menstrual Disorders** — Diagnosis and management of irregular periods, PCOS, and endometriosis
- **Menopause Management** — Hormone therapy and lifestyle counseling
- **Gynecological Ultrasound** — Transvaginal and transabdominal imaging

## Your Comfort Is Our Priority

We understand the sensitivity of gynecological care. Our clinics feature private consultation rooms, female-only waiting areas on request, and extended appointment times so you never feel rushed.
