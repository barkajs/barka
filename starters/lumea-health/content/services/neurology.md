## Neurological Care

Our neurology department provides expert diagnosis and management of conditions affecting the brain, spinal cord, and peripheral nervous system. Using advanced neuroimaging and electrophysiological testing, we deliver precise diagnoses and effective treatment plans.

## Services

- **Headache & Migraine Clinic** — Specialized assessment and preventive strategies
- **Epilepsy Management** — EEG monitoring and medication optimization
- **Stroke Prevention** — Risk assessment and preventive care programs
- **Movement Disorders** — Parkinson's disease and essential tremor management
- **Peripheral Neuropathy** — Nerve conduction studies and treatment
- **Memory Assessment** — Cognitive screening and early dementia evaluation
