## Advanced Diagnostics

Our diagnostic center is equipped with modern imaging and laboratory technology. Most results are available within 24 hours, and our specialists will help you understand your results thoroughly.

## Available Diagnostics

- **Laboratory Tests** — Full blood panels, hormonal profiles, tumor markers, allergy testing
- **Ultrasound** — Abdominal, thyroid, breast, musculoskeletal, and obstetric ultrasound
- **X-ray** — Digital radiography with minimal radiation exposure
- **ECG & Holter** — Cardiac rhythm assessment
- **Spirometry** — Lung function testing
- **Audiometry** — Hearing assessment

## Quick Results

We prioritize efficiency. Routine blood test results are available online within 24 hours. Urgent tests can be processed within 2-4 hours.
