## Expert Heart Care

At Lumea Health, our cardiology department combines cutting-edge diagnostic technology with experienced specialists to provide comprehensive cardiovascular care. Whether you need a routine heart screening or advanced treatment for a complex condition, our team is here to help.

## Our Cardiology Services

- **ECG & Echocardiography** — Non-invasive heart imaging to assess cardiac function and structure
- **Holter Monitoring** — 24-48 hour continuous heart rhythm recording
- **Exercise Stress Testing** — Evaluate heart performance under controlled physical exertion
- **Ambulatory Blood Pressure Monitoring** — Precise 24-hour blood pressure assessment
- **Cardiac Risk Assessment** — Comprehensive evaluation of cardiovascular risk factors
- **Arrhythmia Management** — Diagnosis and treatment of irregular heart rhythms

## When to See a Cardiologist

You should consider a cardiology consultation if you experience chest pain, shortness of breath, palpitations, dizziness, or if you have a family history of heart disease. Early detection is key to preventing serious cardiovascular events.

## Our Cardiologists

Our cardiology team includes specialists with over 15 years of experience in interventional and preventive cardiology. They regularly participate in international conferences and follow the latest European Society of Cardiology guidelines.
