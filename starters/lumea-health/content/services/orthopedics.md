## Musculoskeletal Excellence

Lumea Health's orthopedic department treats the full spectrum of bone, joint, and muscle conditions. Our multidisciplinary team of orthopedic surgeons, sports medicine specialists, and physiotherapists work together to restore your mobility and quality of life.

## Our Orthopedic Services

- **Joint Pain Diagnosis** — Comprehensive assessment using X-ray, ultrasound, and MRI
- **Sports Medicine** — Injury prevention, treatment, and return-to-sport programs
- **Spine Disorders** — Back pain, herniated discs, and spinal stenosis management
- **Arthroscopy Consultations** — Minimally invasive joint surgery planning
- **Osteoporosis Screening** — DEXA bone density scanning and prevention programs
- **Custom Orthotics** — Individually fitted supports and braces

## Rehabilitation

Our integrated rehabilitation center offers physiotherapy, hydrotherapy, and exercise-based recovery programs tailored to each patient's needs and goals.
