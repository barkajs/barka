## Invest in Prevention

Regular health check-ups are the cornerstone of preventive medicine. Lumea Health offers structured screening packages designed to detect potential health issues before they become serious.

## Check-Up Packages

### Basic Check-Up — 350 PLN
- Blood panel (CBC, glucose, lipids, liver, kidney function)
- Blood pressure and BMI assessment
- General physician consultation

### Executive Check-Up — 890 PLN
- Complete Basic package plus:
- Thyroid function tests
- Cardiac markers
- Abdominal ultrasound
- ECG
- Specialist consultation

### Premium Check-Up — 1,490 PLN
- Complete Executive package plus:
- Chest X-ray
- Exercise stress test
- Urological/gynecological screening
- Dermatological skin check
- Detailed health report with recommendations
