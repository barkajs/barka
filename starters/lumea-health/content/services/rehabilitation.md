## Recovery & Rehabilitation

Our rehabilitation department helps patients recover from injuries, surgeries, and chronic conditions through individualized treatment programs combining manual therapy, exercise, and modern modalities.

## Programs

- **Post-Surgical Rehabilitation** — Hip, knee, shoulder, and spine surgery recovery
- **Sports Rehabilitation** — Return-to-sport programs for athletes
- **Chronic Pain Management** — Multidisciplinary approach to persistent pain
- **Neurological Rehabilitation** — Stroke and brain injury recovery
- **Spinal Rehabilitation** — Back and neck pain treatment programs
- **Manual Therapy** — Osteopathic and physiotherapeutic manual techniques
