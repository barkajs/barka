## Healthcare From Home

Lumea Health's telemedicine service connects you with our doctors through secure video consultations. Perfect for follow-up appointments, medication reviews, and non-emergency consultations.

## How It Works

1. **Book Online** — Schedule a video appointment through our patient portal
2. **Connect** — Join the video call at your scheduled time from any device
3. **Consult** — Discuss your concerns with an experienced Lumea Health physician
4. **Follow Up** — Receive e-prescriptions, referrals, or lab orders electronically

## Available Services

- General consultations and follow-ups
- Prescription renewals and medication reviews
- Specialist consultations (dermatology, endocrinology, neurology)
- Mental health consultations
- Sick leave certificates
