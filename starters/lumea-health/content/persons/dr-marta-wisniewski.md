Dr. Marta Wiśniewska is a board-certified dermatologist with expertise in dermoscopy and early skin cancer detection. She divides her time between Lumea Health Wrocław and Warsaw Centrum clinics.

She trained at Wrocław Medical University and completed advanced dermoscopy training in Barcelona. She is passionate about skin cancer prevention and regularly conducts free screening events.
