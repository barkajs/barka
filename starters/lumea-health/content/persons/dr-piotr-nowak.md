Dr. Piotr Nowak is an orthopedic surgeon specializing in sports medicine and minimally invasive joint surgery. Based at Lumea Health Kraków, he treats athletes and active patients with a focus on rapid recovery and return to activity.

He trained at Jagiellonian University and completed a fellowship in sports medicine at a leading center in Munich. He is the team physician for several professional sports organizations in the Małopolska region.
