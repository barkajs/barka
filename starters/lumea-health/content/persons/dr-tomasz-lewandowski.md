Dr. Tomasz Lewandowski is an endocrinologist with 16 years of experience in managing diabetes, thyroid disorders, and metabolic syndrome. He is a strong advocate for technology in diabetes care, helping patients adopt continuous glucose monitoring and insulin pump therapy.

Based primarily at Lumea Health Wrocław, he also consults at Warsaw Centrum twice a month.
