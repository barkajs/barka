Dr. Anna Kowalska is the Head of Cardiology at Lumea Health and one of the most experienced cardiologists in our network. She completed her medical degree at the Medical University of Warsaw and her cardiology specialization at the Institute of Cardiology in Anin.

With over 18 years of clinical experience, Dr. Kowalska specializes in non-invasive cardiac imaging, including echocardiography and cardiac CT. She is a Fellow of the European Society of Cardiology and regularly presents at international conferences.

Dr. Kowalska is known for her thorough, patient-centered approach. She takes the time to explain conditions and treatment options clearly, ensuring her patients feel informed and empowered in their healthcare decisions.
