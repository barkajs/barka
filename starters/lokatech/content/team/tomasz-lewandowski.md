Tomasz Lewandowski oversees LokaTech's commercial operations across all European markets, managing relationships with Fortune 500 clients and strategic technology partnerships. With 18+ years of experience in enterprise technology sales, Tomasz has a proven track record of building long-term client partnerships that grow from initial engagements into multi-year strategic relationships. Before joining LokaTech in 2017, he spent eight years at Microsoft's Enterprise Services division, where he managed a portfolio exceeding €50M in annual revenue across the DACH and Central European regions.

Tomasz's philosophy is that the best sales in professional services come from delivering exceptional results, not from pitch decks. Under his leadership, LokaTech's client retention rate exceeds 92%, and the average client relationship spans 4.5 years. He holds an MBA from Warsaw School of Economics (SGH) and speaks fluent Polish, German, and English — a combination that has been instrumental in LokaTech's expansion into the DACH market. Tomasz is also responsible for LokaTech's technology partnerships with AWS, Microsoft, and Google Cloud, ensuring the company maintains advanced partner status across all major cloud providers.
