Katarzyna Kamińska leads LokaTech's product strategy and innovation initiatives, driving the company's investment in emerging technologies and new service offerings. With over 10 years of experience in product management and technology strategy, she brings a product-thinking mindset to LokaTech's services business — ensuring that every client engagement starts with a clear understanding of user needs and business outcomes before jumping to technical solutions.

Before LokaTech, Katarzyna served as Senior Product Lead at Allegro, Poland's largest e-commerce platform, where she managed the search and recommendation product line serving 20 million monthly active users. She holds a Master's degree in Human-Computer Interaction from the University of Warsaw and is a certified Product Manager (CPM). At LokaTech, she oversees the Innovation Labs program — a quarterly initiative where cross-functional teams prototype solutions to emerging client challenges using technologies like generative AI, digital twins, and edge computing. Several Innovation Labs projects have evolved into production client engagements, generating over €5M in new revenue since the program's inception.
