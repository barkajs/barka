Jan Nowak joined LokaTech as Chief Technology Officer in 2014, bringing 15+ years of experience in software architecture and distributed systems. Before LokaTech, Jan served as a Principal Software Architect at Google's Zurich office, where he worked on large-scale distributed data processing systems serving billions of requests daily. His research background in consensus algorithms and fault-tolerant systems directly informs LokaTech's approach to building mission-critical enterprise platforms.

Jan holds a PhD in Distributed Systems from AGH University of Krakow and has published over 20 papers in peer-reviewed journals and conferences. At LokaTech, he leads the technical vision across all practice areas, oversees the architecture review board, and drives the company's technology radar — a quarterly assessment of emerging technologies and their readiness for enterprise adoption. He is passionate about mentoring the next generation of software architects and runs LokaTech's internal Architecture Guild, a community of practice where senior engineers share knowledge and debate design decisions.
