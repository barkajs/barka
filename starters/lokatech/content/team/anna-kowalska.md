Anna Kowalska co-founded LokaTech Solutions in 2008 and has led the company from a five-person startup to an 800+ engineer enterprise IT services firm. With over 20 years of experience in the technology industry, Anna brings a rare combination of deep technical understanding and strategic business acumen. Before founding LokaTech, she served as Vice President of Technology Services at Accenture's Central European division, where she managed a portfolio of 40+ enterprise clients across banking, manufacturing, and telecommunications.

Anna holds an MBA from INSEAD and a Master's degree in Computer Science from the Warsaw University of Technology. She is a frequent speaker at technology leadership conferences across Europe and serves on the advisory board of the Polish Technology Industry Association. Under her leadership, LokaTech has been recognized as a Clutch Top 100 IT Services Company, a Great Place to Work, and one of the fastest-growing technology firms in Central Europe. Anna's vision for LokaTech is simple: build a company where exceptional engineers want to work and where clients see a genuine partner, not just a vendor.
