Maria Wiśniewska leads LokaTech's engineering organization of 600+ developers, architects, and QA engineers across five development centers. With over 12 years of experience in engineering leadership, Maria specializes in scaling engineering organizations while maintaining the quality standards and engineering culture that attract top talent. Before LokaTech, she served as Engineering Director at Spotify's Platform division in Stockholm, where she managed 15 engineering squads building the internal developer platform used by 1,500+ engineers.

Maria's approach to engineering leadership centers on platform engineering and developer experience. At LokaTech, she has championed the adoption of Internal Developer Platforms, standardized CI/CD pipelines, and engineering metrics that measure team health alongside delivery velocity. She holds a Master's degree in Software Engineering from Wrocław University of Technology and is an active contributor to the platform engineering community, speaking regularly at conferences including PlatformCon, QCon, and DevOpsDays. Her team has reduced onboarding time for new engineers from four weeks to five days through investments in documentation, automation, and structured mentoring.
