Maria Wisniewska leads KadoServices' recruitment operations, managing a team of 200+ recruiters across all industry verticals — manufacturing, logistics, IT, finance, retail, and healthcare. She is responsible for recruitment methodology, quality standards, recruiter training, and the development of new service lines.

Maria joined KadoServices in 2013 as a Senior Recruiter specializing in finance and shared services. Her ability to build deep candidate relationships and consistently deliver high-quality placements earned her rapid promotions — from Team Leader (2015) to Recruitment Director (2018) to VP Recruitment (2021).

Before KadoServices, Maria spent six years at Hays Poland, where she built the company's finance and SSC recruitment desk in Kraków from scratch, growing it to a team of 12 consultants. Her early career in recruitment gave her first-hand experience of every aspect of the hiring process — from cold-calling passive candidates to negotiating complex offer packages.

Maria holds a Master's degree in Psychology from Jagiellonian University, which gives her a distinctive edge in candidate assessment. She is a certified assessor for several psychometric tools (SHL, Hogan, MBTI) and has designed the competency-based interview frameworks used across KadoServices. She is also a frequent contributor to industry publications, writing about talent acquisition trends, candidate experience, and recruitment technology.

Under Maria's leadership, KadoServices has achieved a 94% retention rate for permanent placements and has grown the RPO practice from zero to 35+ active programs.
