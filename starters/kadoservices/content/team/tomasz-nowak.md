Tomasz Nowak joined KadoServices in 2011 as Operations Manager and was appointed Chief Operating Officer in 2017. He oversees all service delivery operations — temporary staffing, permanent recruitment, payroll, and on-site management — across the company's 12 offices in Poland.

Before KadoServices, Tomasz spent eight years at ManpowerGroup, where he managed operations for industrial staffing in the Silesia region. He built his career on the front lines of workforce management — coordinating hundreds of temporary workers across manufacturing plants, solving staffing emergencies at 3 AM, and developing the operational processes that enable consistent service delivery at scale.

At KadoServices, Tomasz has been instrumental in building the operational infrastructure that supports 15,000+ placements per year. He designed the company's quality management system, implemented the ATS (Applicant Tracking System) and workforce management platforms, and established the on-site coordination model that now operates at over 30 client locations.

Tomasz holds a degree in Industrial Engineering from the Silesian University of Technology and an MBA from Kozminski University. He is a certified Lean Six Sigma Black Belt and has applied process improvement methodologies to reduce time-to-fill by 40% and improve candidate quality scores by 25% since taking the COO role. He is passionate about using technology and data to improve staffing operations while never losing sight of the human element at the center of the business.
